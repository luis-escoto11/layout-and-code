How it Works

Your website is an investment, it requires time and dedication. I believe that with a clear and structured process, and effective communication, great results are achieved.

  1. 1
    Know your Business

    What kind of business do you have?
    What services does your business provide?
    I collect this information, so I can create a proposal that meets your business description.

  2. 2
    Development

    It all begins through sketches for each element, in this way I establish a visual and clear hierarchy. Then, I proceed to code and build your website.

  3. 3
    Publish your Site

    Once I've finished the design, and you are satisfied with it, I proceed to upload all the files to your server. Now everybody can visit your website. Hooray!
